BitesNearU — Food that would go to waste, saved & shared locally

Affordable near-expiry food from local cafes, restaurants and supermarkets. Quick pickups shown on a map — save money and help cut food waste.

  • Save money — discounted ready-made meals
  • Reduce food waste — help the environment
  • Support local shops — extra revenue for surplus food

Live map with local pick-up spots

How BitesNearU works

1. Shops list surplus

Restaurants, cafes and supermarkets post discounted items that are nearing expiry and specify pickup windows.

2. You grab & collect

Find nearby options on the map, reserve or pay in-app and pick up within the time window.

3. Earn & track

Earn loyalty points, track your community sustainability impact, and help reduce carbon footprint.
